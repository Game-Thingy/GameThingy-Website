About The Game

Game Thingy is a 2D mining game where you go mining underground in search of valuable gems and minerals. While mining for gems and minerals, you may come across minesafts along the way. In the minesafts, you may find treasure chests which may contain valuable treasure. You can earn coins by mining mineral and gems . With the coins you've earned, you use them to buy upgrades like better pickaxes and so on.

System Requirements

Any system that is from the last 10 - 15 years.
